Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver outside Kadapa?+

Yes! We deliver across Andhra Pradesh and can ship pan-India for select hampers. WhatsApp us for delivery details outside Kadapa city limits and we'll confirm availability and timelines for your area.

Can I customise a hamper?+

Absolutely! Custom hampers are one of our specialties. Simply WhatsApp us with your occasion, budget, and preferences — we'll put together something beautiful and unique just for you.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), NEFT/IMPS bank transfers, and cash on delivery for orders within Kadapa. Payment details are shared via WhatsApp after order confirmation.

How soon will my order be delivered?+

For Kadapa city orders placed before 2 PM, we guarantee same day delivery. For custom hampers or bulk orders, we typically need 24–48 hours for preparation. We'll always confirm the timeline via WhatsApp.

Do you do bulk or corporate orders?+

Yes, we love corporate gifting! We offer special pricing for bulk orders (10+). Ideal for Diwali, New Year, employee gifting, and event hampers. Contact us on WhatsApp for a custom quote.
